Kuvasz Properties / Mentality

Kuvasz is very cuddly with a great need for social contact, although it may be reserved for new acquaintances, who are connected to its guard instinct. It is a brave dog who does everything to defend his herd. Proper environmental education is therefore necessary for it to work well in today's society. Kuvasz likes to think independently and solve problems himself, which is why it is not certain that he always wants to do as his master / mistress thinks. The young dog can be perceived as difficult while an adult is much calmer.
